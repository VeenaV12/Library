Karmayogi


M.S Ashokan

"Karmayogi" is a biography of E. Sreedharan, affectionately known as the "Metro Man" of India. Authored by M.S. Ashokan, the book chronicles Sreedharan's remarkable journey from a humble engineer to an iconic figure renowned for his leadership in transforming India's infrastructure, particularly through the successful implementation of the Delhi Metro project. Sreedharan's unwavering dedication, integrity, and commitment to excellence serve as inspiration for aspiring engineers and leaders alike, making "Karmayogi" a captivating tale of vision and perseverance.

About the Author

M. S. Ashokan is an accomplished author known for his insightful biographies and engaging storytelling. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for unraveling the lives of eminent personalities, Ashokan's works captivate readers, offering intimate glimpses into the lives and achievements of his subjects.
