Njan


N.N Pillai

Njan (meaning "I" in Malayalam) is the autobiography of N. N. Pillai, a celebrated playwright and Indian Independence activist. It's not just about his career in theatre, but a coming-of-age story that follows his adventures as a young man seeking opportunity abroad. The book delves into his experiences in Malaya (now Malaysia) during World War II, including his time with the Indian National Army (INA).

About the Author

N. N. Pillai (1918-1995) was a true titan of Malayalam theatre. Earning the title "Nadakacharyan," he wasn't just an actor, but also a playwright, director, and even a freedom fighter in the Indian National Army. He founded his own drama troupe, Viswa Kerala Kala Samithi, which staged his plays for decades. Pillai also acted in films, including some based on his own plays.
