Wings of Fire:An Autobiography

A.P.J Abdul Kalam

"Wings of Fire" is the autobiography of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, a former president and influential scientist in India. It tells the story of his remarkable journey, from a humble childhood to leading India's space and missile programs. Through his personal experiences, the book offers valuable lessons about perseverance, hard work, and the importance of dreams.

About the Author

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who wore two hats. He played a pivotal role in developing India's missile and space programs, earning him the nickname "Missile Man." Later, he served as the 11th president of India (2002-2007) and was known for his humility and connection to the people.
