Steve Jobs


Walter Issacson

Steve Jobs: The biography explores the life of the iconic co-founder of Apple. It delves into his journey, from being an adopted child to a college dropout, and finally to building a technological empire. The book sheds light on his passion for design, his relentless drive, and the visionary thinking that led to groundbreaking products like the iPhone and Macintosh.

About the Author

Walter Isaacson is a biographer who chronicles the lives of influential figures. He's penned best-selling biographies on Albert Einstein, Benjamin Franklin, Steve Jobs, and Leonardo da Vinci. His work delves into the qualities that shaped these icons and the impact they had on their respective fields.
