Nakshathrangalude Snehabhajanam


M.K Sanu

"Nakshathrangalude Snehabhajanam" (Beloved of the Stars), written by M. K. Sanu, delves into the life of the celebrated Malayalam poet Changampuzha Krishna Pillai. It's not just a biography; it's a nuanced exploration of the poet's emotional complexities and the struggles that informed his powerful verses.

About the Author

M. K. Sanu is a prolific Malayalam writer, critic, and social activist. Born in 1926, he has worn many hats in his life - professor, biographer, journalist, and even orator. Sanu is a champion for human rights and has authored over 36 books on various subjects. He is a recipient of several prestigious awards including the Kerala Sahitya Akademi Award and the Kendra Sahitya Akademi Award.
