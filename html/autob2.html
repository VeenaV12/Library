Kannerum Kinavum


V.T Bhattathirippadu

Kanneerum Kinavum, meaning "Tears and Dreams" in Malayalam, is the celebrated autobiography of V.T. Bhattathiripad, a social reformer from Kerala, India. The book narrates his transformation from a young man raised for priesthood to a radical writer and activist. It delves into the struggles of breaking free from social conventions and his fight for a more just society.

About the Author

V.T. Bhattathiripad was a prominent Indian social reformer, playwright, and independence activist. He bravely challenged the caste system and conservatism within his own Namboothiri community, working to dismantle outdated traditions. His writings, including the play "Adukkalayail Ninnu Arangathekku" and his autobiography "Kanneerum Kinavum" (Tears and Dreams), are considered significant works of Malayalam literature.
