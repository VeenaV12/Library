The Story of My Experiments with Truth


M.K Gandhi

The Story of My Experiments with Truth is the autobiography of Mahatma Gandhi, leader of India's independence movement. It's not just a life story, but a chronicle of his lifelong pursuit of truth. Gandhi explores his moral and spiritual awakening, detailing his experiments with non-violence and his fight for justice.

About the Author

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, also known as Mahatma Gandhi ("Great Soul"), was the leader of India's independence movement against British rule. He famously employed nonviolent resistance ( Satyagraha) and civil disobedience to achieve his goals. Gandhi's philosophy of peace and social justice continues to inspire movements for change around the world.
