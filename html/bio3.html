I Came Upon a Lighthouse


Shantanu Naidu

"I Came Upon a Lighthouse" is a memoir by Shantanu Naidu about his unexpected friendship with Ratan Tata, a respected Indian businessman. Naidu, a young engineer, meets Tata and their connection leads to mentorship, business partnership, and a heartwarming bond. The book offers a unique perspective on Tata and explores themes of inspiration and guidance.

About the Author

The author of "I Came Upon a Lighthouse" is Shantanu Naidu. It's a memoir detailing his unique relationship with Ratan Tata, a respected Indian industrialist. Naidu, a young engineer, impressed Tata with a safety invention for stray dogs. This led to an unexpected friendship between the two, which Naidu chronicles in the book.
